Is This You?

This guide is for church leaders who...

  • See growing mental and emotional health needs in their congregation
  • Want to care well for people but don't have a clear process in place
  • Worry their pastors and staff are carrying too much
  • Know they need trained lay leaders but aren't sure where to start
  • Are looking for a Christ-centered approach that goes beyond a single program
  • Want to respond with both compassion and wisdom when people reach out

You don't need to become a counseling center. You don't need licensed professionals on staff. What you need is a clear, sustainable pathway so that when someone in your church reaches out for help, you can respond with confidence.

This guide will help you see where you are today, where the gaps exist, and what a next step forward looks like for your church.
